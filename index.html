Who Are We?

To The Youths
We see the need to develop leadership in young people through exchange experiences. We do this by providing unique volunteering and internship opportunities.
You can be at the core of your experience and learn on the go. There are numerous chances to use your abilities and acquire more.

Who Are We?

To organizations
AIESEC shapes young people with the skills that are needed to create solutions in this fast-paced world. Grow your company by enabling young people. AIESEC develops young people all over the world with challenging roles and practical experiences.
